_.o0 Paramount Warlocks 0o._
==========================

![Griffin](http://i.imgur.com/Q6CcCt8.png)

Isometric 2D turn-based strategy HTML5/Javascript game. 

You take control of a warlock and his army and battle against enemy AI or human player.
